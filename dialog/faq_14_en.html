Definition
In Teon, many items and resources are tradable. Player can trade these items and gold with other players.

How to Trade
1. Start a trade
Tap on the other character's portrait and select [Trade] in the drop-down menu.
2. Add items
Drag and drop an item onto the trade window and you can set the trade item and item amount.
3. Lock items
Once locked, the items in the trade window cannot be changed again. Now wait for the other character to lock items.
4. Confirm trade
When both characters have locked and checked the items in the trade window, they can complete the trade by taping the confirm button.
5. Cancel a trade
Close the trade window to cancel the trade.

Important Notice
Any trade completed in the game is irreversible. We strongly recommend players to pay close attention while trading with others to avoid unnecessary losses.
If the trade fails and your bag is full, the overweighted items will drop to the ground.
