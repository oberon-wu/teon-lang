Quest Target:
Give one Earth Ore, Wind Ore, Fire Ore or Water Ore to Apocalypser [Element Enchant] in Dartford Town.

Target Guiding:
Apocalypser [Element Enchant] is in Dartford Town.

Quest Description:
Apocalypser [Element Enchant] needs you accept her test so that you are qualified enough to receive the intelligence from her. To get the intelligence, you need to bring one elemental Ore to her.
