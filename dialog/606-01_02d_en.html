Quest Target:
Give Lump of Dark Mithril x1 to Dark Mithril Collector.

Target Guiding:
Dark Mithril Collector is in Dartford Town.


Quest Description:
Chamber of Commerce is delegating qualified adventurers to collect Lump of Dark Mithril. You can give Lump of Dark Mithril x1 to Dark Mithril Collector so as to exchange for experience, Advanced Heroic Potion and EXP Cube(120% EXP Buff).
