Pure soul attracts Wraiths. They are easy to infest with evil spirits.

After Galahad died, he absorb millions of Wraiths in Maros Swamps. Under the impetus of evil, he climbed out of the graveyard with curse and death. The soldiers who accompanied him become his most faithful subordinates of the undead. Under the leadership of this death knight, they occupied the whole Maros Dungeon.
