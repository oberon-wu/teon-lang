Random Teleport
You can teleport via items or skills. The destination cannot be selected.
Item: Scroll of Teleportation.
Skill: Teleport.

Controlled Teleport
How to teleport: Use the teleportation item or skill and select a destination marked in the bookmark to teleport.
Item: Blessed Scroll of Teleportation, Ring of Teleport Control and scroll of a specific place (e.g. Scroll of Escape).
Skill: Use the skill Teleport while wearing a Ring of Teleportation.
Bookmarks: Provides the coordination of locations.
