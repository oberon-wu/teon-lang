Teon Pass Pre-Season Notice:
1. Teon Pass Pre-Season(TP) level is based on the TP Points that each TP level needed.
2. Each TP level needs 1000 TP Points.
3. The TP Points can acquire from Basic daily quests and Advanced daily quests.
4. The TP Points can redeem from TP Point Note in Teon Pass interface.
5. Basic daily quests can be accepted by any character and without limitation. And the Basic daily quest TP Points rewards are doubled during weekend.
6. Advanced daily quests are only available for adventurers who purchase the Advanced Teon Pass.
7. Basic daily quests require characters to have items TP Basic Permit and TP Daily Quest Permit (Quest) to activate the first step of the basic daily quest.
8. Advanced daily quests require characters to have items TP Advanced Permit and TP Advanced Daily Quest Permit (Quest) to activate the first step of the advanced daily quest.
9. Both TP Daily Quest Permit and TP Advanced Daily Quest Permit are only available to purchase once per day and will be reset the purchase limit at 00:00 on Teon Server Time. (Teon Server Time is available on our official website at www.teonworld.com).
10. Both TP Daily Quest Permit and TP Advanced Daily Quest Permit can purchase from Orianna.
11. Basic daily quests can be accepted from Orianna which is located in Maros Town.
12. Advanced daily quests can be accepted from Vina which is located in Maros Town.
13. The duration of Teon Pass is from August 10th, 2021 to September 11th, 2021.
