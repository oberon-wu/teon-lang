Suggest 1
STR:8, CON:16, DEX:7, WIS:18, INT:18, CHA:8.

Advantage: High spell power, magic resistance and critical damage
Disadvantage: Low AC
Tips: Classic build, will have high magic damage during the late gameplay, at the same time need to pay attention to self-protection.
Weapon Recommendation: Staff


Suggest 2
STR:8, CON:18, DEX:7, WIS:18, INT:16, CHA:8.

Advantage: High HP and magic resistance
Disadvantage: Low AC
Tips: Defensive build, giving up attacking ability to increase surviving ability, able to strengthen magical ability during the late gameplay, encouraged to be good at balancing both attack and defence spells.
Weapon Recommendation: Staff


Suggest 3
STR:13, CON:16, DEX:7, WIS:13, INT:18, CHA:8.

Advantage: High spell power and physical damage
Disadvantage: Low AC and magic resistance
Tips: Semi-magic build, will perform physical damage most of the time, able to strengthen its magic ability during the late gameplay, encouraged to be good at both physical and magic damage.
Weapon Recommendation: Staff
