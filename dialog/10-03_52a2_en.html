An Oath is the only pass for players to travel across The Land of Edin.

How to obtain an Oath：
1.Purchase and use Sealed Oath[15Days], to receive 15 days membership.
2. Free Oath (limited-time events or freebies)
3. Consignment House
Note: Oaths of different types are not stackable. If you use them at the same time,
they will be consumed. Oaths of the same type are stackable.

Areas in the Land of Edin：

1.Areas which required an oath to enter: Old Escape Tunnel, Maros, Elven Garden, Orc Town, Orcish Guild, Sandwood, and future new maps.

2.Areas which doesn't required an oath to enter: Edin Sky Island, Promise Island, Promise Island Dugen F1 and F2.
