Quest:
Defeat Skeleton Mage or other skeleton type monsters, collect 150 Skeleton Staff and give to Karol [Warehouse].

Guide:
Skeleton Mage is to the North of Maros Town.

Karol [Warehouse] is in Maros Town.

Description:
Skeleton Mage is fond of magic, and they are not afraid of the magical barrier. So, we need to defeat them and grab the staff from their hands.
