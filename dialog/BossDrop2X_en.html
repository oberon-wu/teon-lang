BOSS Drop Double Chances Event


Event Date


2020.3.26 to 2020.4.21

Event Overview


During the event, everyMonday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday defeat Specify BOSS between18:00:00 to 23:59:59, drop probability of some rare items will be greatly increased!

Event BOSS and drop rate promotion items list


Monday and Tuesday 18:00:00 to 23:59:59


Bran：Cursed Sword of Bran drop rate increases!
Baphomet：Mana Stone, Cursed Staff of Baphomet and [Glowing Aura]Spell Book drop rate increases!
Pincer：Star Dust Earrings and [Brave Aura]Spell Book drop rate increases!

Thursday and Friday 18:00:00 to 23:59:59


Death Knight：Sun Flame Earrings, Crimson Cloth and Badge of Knight drop rate increases!
Deathsting：Giant Scorpion Venom and [Brave Aura]Spell Book drop rate increases!
Drake：Branch of Yumi, Mana Stone and [Brave Aura]Spell Book drop rate increases!

Tips：
Only defeat the designated boss of the day within the designated time(18:00:00 to 23:59:59), can increase the drop rate of the listed items, but not count on the refresh time of Boss.
