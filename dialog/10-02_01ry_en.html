1. You can put the items from your bag and spells that you have learned into the Hotkey Button
2. Click the Hotkey Button to use your item immediately
3. You can also use Hotkey Button to switch between weapons and armors
4. The two Hotkey Button with the decorative borders are theSelf-casting Hotkey, and only take effect to your sole character when being clicked
5. The Toggle Button (below the time) can be use to select NPC, your character, other players or switch between targets.
