1. Every 4 hours in real world are equal to 1 day in the game.
2. During the night, the player's vision will be affected by the changing lighting condition.
3. During the night, you can use lighting tools such as candles and lanterns to extend your view distance.
4. Elves have night vision. During the night, an Elf's vision will turn green. You can turn this feature off in "Settings".
5. During the night, undead creatures become more active and deal increased damage.
