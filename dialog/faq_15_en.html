Equipment Types
Weapons
Types: Sword, Spear, Bludgeon, Staff, Dagger, Bow.
Differences: There are one-handed and two-handed weapons. While wielding a one-handed weapon, you can also use a shield. Two-handed weapons and shields cannot be equipped at the same time. (Given that your class is able to use a shield)
Differences in attack speed: The attack speed of different weapon types are also different. For example, daggers are much faster than bludgeons.
Armors
Helmet, Shirt, Body Armor, Cloak, Shoes, Shield, Gloves.
Accessories
Amulet, Belt, Ring, Earrings.

Equipment Set
Some equipments are set to be a set. After wearing a set of equipment, an extra effect or attribute can be added.

Special Effects
Some equipment may have special effects. For instance, Staff of Mana has an additional effect that allows the character to steal mana from enemy.

Status
A piece of equipment may have Normal, Cursed, or Blessed status.
Normal: Common equipment.
Cursed: Cannot be unequipped once put on unless using Scroll of Curse Removal.
Blessed: Blessed weapons deal additional damage to certain monsters; Blessed armor and accessories additionally reduces the character's damage taken from certain monsters.
