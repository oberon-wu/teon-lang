Equipment Types
Weapons:
Types: Sword, Blunt, Spear, Dagger, Staff, Bow, Claws, Dual Blades, Gauntlet.
Differences: There are one-handed and two-handed weapons. While wielding a one-handed weapon, you can also euqip a shield (If your class is able to equip a shield). Two-handed weapons and shields cannot be equipped at the same time.
Different types of weapon have different attack speeds. For example, daggers are much faster than bludgeons.
Armors:
Helmet, Cloak, Body Armor, Doublet, Gloves, Shoes, Shield.
Accessories:
Earrings, Necklace, Belt, Ring.

Equipment Set
Some equipments are in a set. After wearing a whole set of equipment, an extra effect or attribute can be added.

Special Effects
Some equipment may have special effects. For example, Staff of Mana has an additional effect that allows the character to steal mana from enemy.

Status
A piece of equipment may have Normal, Cursed, or Blessed status.
Normal: Common equipment.
Cursed: Cannot be unequipped once equipped, unless using Scroll of Curse Removal.
Blessed: Blessed weapons deal additional damage to certain monsters; Blessed armor and accessories reduce additional damage taken from certain monsters.
