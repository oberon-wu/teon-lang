How to equip your weapon/armor:



1. Tap Character Icon to reach Main Menu, and then tap [Bag]
2. Tap on the weapon/armor you would like to equip and tap [Use].

Notice:
1. You can't equip the armor if you had already equipped the same part. E.g. if you had already equipped a pair of gloves, please tap [Use] to take off the current one before equipped another.
2. Please take off these three part of armors in the order of cloak, armor, and shirt.
