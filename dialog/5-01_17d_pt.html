Quest:
Collect 10 Venom Spear for Harbert.

Guide:
Harbert is inside Damelot City
Elite Skeleton Spearmen are in the northeast from Damelot City.

Description:
Harbert is afraid that you are not able to defend the city all by yourself. Collect 10 Venom Spears for him from the Elite Skeleton Spearmen so he can arm some troops.
