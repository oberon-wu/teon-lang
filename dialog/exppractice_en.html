EXP Gaining System Info
1.Adventurer can buy Elder's Note form NPC Joanna, all of class can buy and use it.
2.Expired time of Elder's Note Bag is 24 hours, it will be disappear when it expired, please inform that.
3.Effective time of EXP Gaining is 4 hour, you can acquire a certain amount of EXP after 4 hours.
4.EXP required from EXP Gaining system is related to character's level. The higher level character has will get less EXP gaining.
