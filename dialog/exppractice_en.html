EXP Gaining System Info
1.Adventurer can buy Elder's Note form Joanna.
2.All of class can buy it and use it.
2.Expired time of Elder's Note Bag is 24 hours, it will be disappear when it expired, please inform that.
3.Effective time of EXP Gaining when using Elder's Note Bag is 4 hour, you can acquire a certain amount of EXP after 4 hours.
4.Effective time of EXP Gaining when using Elder's Advanced Note Bag is 1 hour, the total amount of EXP you can acquire is the same as using Elder's Note Bag in 4 hours.
5.The EXP acquired from using Elder's Note Bag is related to character's level. The higher level of the character has, the less EXP will gain from the system.
6.The EXP acquired from using Elder's Advanced Note Bag is not related to character's level. You will always acquire the same amount of EXP from the system.
7.The EXP Gaining System is only working in Maros Town, leaving the town or logging out your character will pause the effective time count down.
8.Character who reach the maximum level will not able to use EXP Gaining System.
