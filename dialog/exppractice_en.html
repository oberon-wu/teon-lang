EXP Gaining System Info
1.Adventurer can buy Elder's Note form Joanna.
2.All of class can buy it and use it.
2.Expired time of Elder's Note Bag is 24 hours, it will be disappear when it expired, please inform that.
3.Effective time of EXP Gaining when using Elder's Note Bag is 4 hour, you can acquire a certain amount of EXP after 4 hours.
4.Effective time of EXP Gaining when using Elder's Advanced Note Bag is 1 hour, you can acquire the same amount of EXP with using Elder's Note Bag after 1 hours.
5.EXP acquired from EXP Gaining system is related to character's level. The higher level of the character has, the less EXP will gain from the system.
6.The EXP gaining reduction from clause 6 is only affect the Elder's Note Bag.
7.The EXP Gaining System is only working in Maros Town, please do not leave the town during the effective time.
8.Character who reach the maximum level will not able to use EXP Gaining System.
