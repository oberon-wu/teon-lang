Xp Growing System Info
1.Adventurer can buy Note form NPC Joanna, each class can buy and use it.
2.Expired time of Lucky Bag is 24 hours, it will be disappear when it expired, please know it.
3.Effective time of xp growing is 4 hour, you can acquire a relevant amount of xp after 4 hours.
4.Xp required from xp growing system is related to character's level. The higher level character will get less xp growing.
