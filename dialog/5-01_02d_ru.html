Quest:
Extract 5 Venom of Scorpion from Scorpions for Gol.

Quest:
Gol is in Sandwood Town
Scorpions can be found in the south from Sandwood Town.

Description:
To kill the thick skinned Basilisks, Gol needs some Venom of Scorpion. Head south from Sandwood town and collect 5 Venom of Scorpion from Scorpions.
