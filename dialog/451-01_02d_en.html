Quest Target:
Bring a +1 Trainee's Armor to Rabia [Armor Shop].

Target Guiding:
Rabia [Armor Shop] is in Maros Town.

Quest Description:
Rabia is instructing to you on how to improve your armor. She asks you to enchant a piece of your armor to +1. If you are not sure what kind of +1 Trainee's Armor you are required, you should speak with Rabia [Armor Shop] again and find out.
