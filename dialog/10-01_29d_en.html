Quest:
Defeat Zombie. Collect 10 Token of the Loved Ones; Defeat Deadly Lotus. Collect 10 Spike Branch for Gordon.

Guide:
Gordon(32675,32830) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the Southwest.
Zombie and Deadly Lotus can be found around Gordon.

Description:
For the safety of the villagers, Gordon ask you to defeat the Zombie and Deadly Lotus.
You should use Scroll of Polymorph, so that they will not actively attacks you.
