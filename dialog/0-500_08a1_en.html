You can shapeshift into a monster, and some aggressive monsters will no longer attack you onsight...well, unless you hit them first.

Take the Ultimate Scroll of Polymorph, and shapeshift into a Death Knight.

Death Knight is the strongest shape among all others and requires level 50 before you can fully control its power.

To use the scroll: Tap Character Portrait > [Bag] > Use the Scroll
