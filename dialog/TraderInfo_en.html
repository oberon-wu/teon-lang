Trade Union manages all trades in Edin.

Otherwise, we provide materials to some organization to saving Edin people.

We also encourage EXP to the adventurer ever help us.

Here are more info:

1. Trade Union set its headquarter in Dartford Castle;

2. With the Hunter Guild Leader introduction, characters can accept collect task of Trade Union;
2.1 Collect tasks provide extra EXP;
2.2 Most of Collect tasks are repeatable cyclic tasks;
2.3 Collect tasks are divided into Animal Collect, Metal Collect, Gem Collect and Rare Metal Collect;
2.4 You have to hand in one collect permit according to the type of task before the task begin;
2.5 Collect Permit selling by all Collect Permit Npc in Dartford Castle and Dartford Town with the limited time and quantity.
2.6 Each Collect Permit has expired time, please finish it in the period of time.
