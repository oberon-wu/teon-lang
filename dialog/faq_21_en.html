Polymorph
Characters can polymorph via items to change their appearance.

Effects
1. When polymorphed into a monster, the character's movement speed will be affected.
2. When polymorphed into a monster, characters of the same class will have different attack speed based on the equipped weapon type.
3. When polymorphed into a monster, characters equipped with weapons of the same type will have different attack speed based on the class of the character.
4. Polymorphed characters will not be active attacked by certain monsters.
5. Polymorphed character may not wear certain equipment.


How to Polymorph



Tap [Character Portrait] on the top-left, then tap the [Polymorph] button in the drop-down menu, and the [Polymorph Monsters List] will be opend.




Tap [Label] label ①, and you can see various polymorph kinds and levels.
Tap [Monster Card] label ②. Check out the monster image and polymorph time in monster attribute label ③.




Tap [View Polymorph Materials] button label ① to check out the required materials for polymorph.
When the materials that character has can fulfill multiple formulas at the same time, the first formula will be used and so on.
Tap [Polymorph] button label ② and character can do the polymorph.




To help players polymorph more quickly, we add a polymorph hotkey.
Tap the [Polymorph Hotkey] button label ①, and a polymorph list label ② will be opened.
Drag and drop [Monster Card] label ③ to the polymorph list label ② and the selected monster can be added to the polymorph list.
To use polymorph quickly during the battle, players can put the needed monster into the polymorph list before the battle.




Drag and drop [Polymorph Hotkey] button label ① to the hotkey button area and it can be added to the hotkey button area.
Tap the hotkey button in hotkey area label ② to open the polymorph list.
Tap the monster inside the polymorph list label ③ to polymorph.


Polymorph Items
1. Polymorph Scrolls
a. Types: There are three kinds of these scrolls, normal, advanced and specail advanced.
Normal Polymorph Scrolls: Normal Polymorph have 3 levels: Lv.1, Lv.2, Lv.3. The character level requirement respectively are: Lv10, Lv20 and Lv30.
Advanced Polymorph Scrolls: Provides special appearances. Available at level 50.
Special Advanced Polymorph Scrolls: Provides special appearances without level limit.
b. Available Appearances:
Lv.1 Polymorph Scroll: Shelob, Elder Mage, Skeleton Swordsman, Skeleton Bowman, Skeleton Spearman.
Lv.2 Polymorph Scroll: Ungoliant, Undead Swordsman, Undead Bowman, Undead Guard, Undead Mage, Blood Wolf.
Lv.3 Polymorph Scroll: Bugbear, Thri Keen, Drow, Orc Mage, Blood Nightshade.
Advanced Polymorph Scroll: Death Knight, Demon Archer, Dark Elder, Arbiter, Baphomet, Succubus Queen.
Special Advanced Polymorph Scroll: Death Knight, Demon Archer, Dark Elder, Arbiter, Baphomet, Succubus Queen.
2. Polymorph Wand
Polymorph Wands can be used on other targets, transforming them into a random appearance.
