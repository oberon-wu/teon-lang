Items Loss Redemption:

1. There is a chance to lose one or more items upon each death. You have the chance to redeem your valuable items.

2. You can redeem your lost items within 120 minutes.

3. Items will be lost permanently (if killed by NPC) or given to PKer if the ransom (Teon Coins) was not paid within 120 minutes. The time will constantly count even the character offline.

4. The ransom for each lost item is paid separately.

5. Part of the ransom you paid goes to the PKer if you were killed by a player.
