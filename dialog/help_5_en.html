Applicable Areas
1. Non-PVP Zones

Name Status
1. Pink name: A temporary status showing that the player has attacked another non-pink or red named player.
2. Red name: This status appears when the player's Lawful Value is at or below -1000.
3. Normal status: Blue or white names indicate the player is in normal status.
4. Display priorities: Red>Pink>Normal

Penalties
1. In normal status, attacking a normal status player will receive a pink-name-penalty for 90 seconds.
2. In pink name status, attacking a normal status player will receive a pink-name-penalty for 90 seconds.
3. In red name status, attacking a normal status player will NOT receive a pink-name-penalty.
4. In any status, attacking a red or pink-named player will NOT receive a pink-name-penalty.

Murder Penalties
1. Killing a normal status player will be punished by justice and lose certain Lawful Value.
2. Killing a normal status player will cause the guards to chase after you for 48 hours (real time).
3. Killing a red or pink named player will not receive any penalty.
