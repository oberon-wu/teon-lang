Quest Target:
Equip a Trainee's Weapon and speak with Pascal [Weapon Shop] again.

Target Guiding:
Pascal [Weapon Shop] is in Maros Town.

Quest Description:
Pascal is instructing to you on how to increase your damage. Your first step is to get yourself a Trainee's Weapon. If you are not sure what kind of Trainee's Weapon you are looking for, you should speak with Pascal [Weapon Shop] again and find out.
