Quest Target:
Hunt Tunnel Series Monsters, collect 10 Ancient King's Scepter and give them to Swain.


Target Guiding:
Swain is at Promise Island Dungeon 2/F.
Tunnel Series Monsters can be found in Old Escape Tunnel.
Talk to Swain and you can enter the Old Escape Tunnel.


Quest Description:
Defeat the monsters in Old Escape Tunnel and collect 10 Ancient King's Scepter.
