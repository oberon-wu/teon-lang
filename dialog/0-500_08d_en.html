Quest:
Alcock gave you Ultimate Deformation Scroll to transform into Death Knight. Complete the transformation and continue talking with Alcock.

Guide:
Alcock is in Medieval Field.Ultimate Deformation Scroll can be found in your bag.

Description:
Alcock let you to use his Ultimate Deformation Scroll, to transform into Death Knight.

Death Knight is the highest type of transformation. Originally, you need to reach level 50 before you can use it. Alcock gives you a special transformation scroll.
