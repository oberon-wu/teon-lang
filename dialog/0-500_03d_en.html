Quest:
Defeat Dwarf Peasant. Collect Dwarven Token x2 and bring back to Milon.

Guide:
Milon(32724,32759) is in Medieval Field.
Dwarf Peasants can be found North from Milon.

Description:
Milon wants you to defeat the Dwarf Peasant which located at his North.

How to attack:

Click the toggle button (Located on the right side of the screen below the clock) to switch between targets, click the attack button to enable auto attack on your enemies.
