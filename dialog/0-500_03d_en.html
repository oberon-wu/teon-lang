Quest:
Defeat Weaken Dwarf. Collect2 Weaken Dwarf Token and bring back to Milon.

Guide:
Milon(32724,32759) is in Medieval Field. Weaken Dwarf can be found at Milon North side.

Description:
Milon wants you to defeat the Weaken Dwarf which located at his Northwest.

How to attack:

Click the toggle button to switch between targets, click the attack button to attack your enemies, when the attack button turns yellow, you'll keep on attacking until your enemy has been defeated.
