Equipment Types
Weapons: Sword, Spear, Bludgeon, Staff, Dagger, Bow.
Armor: Helmet, Doublet, Body Armor, Cloak, Shoes, Shield, Gauntlets.
Accessories: Amulet, Belt, Ring, Earrings.
Set Items: Some gear has set effects. When you wear a whole set of equipment, you will receive additional attributes or effects as reward.

Differences and Effects
Differences between weapons: There are one-handed and two-handed weapons. While wielding a one-handed weapon, you can also use a shield. Two-handed weapons and shields cannot be equipped at the same time. (Given that your class is able to use a shield)
Differences in attack speed: The attack speed of different weapon types are also different. For example, daggers are much faster than bludgeons.
Special effects: Some equipment may have special effects. For instance, Staff of Mana has an additional effect that allows the character to steal mana from enemy.

Status
Type: A piece of equipment may have Normal, Cursed, or Blessed status.
Normal: Common equipment.
Cursed: Cannot be unequipped once put on unless using Scroll of Curse Removal.
Blessed: Blessed weapons deal additional damage to certain monsters; Blessed armor and accessories additionally reduces the character's damage taken from certain monsters.
