Meaning:
Equipment Enchantment is a crucial way to fortify your character. Enchanted equipment provides additional benefits such as increased damage, accuracy and resistances.

Enchanting Materials:
Scroll of Enchant Weapon: used to enchant weapons.
Scroll of Enchant Armor: used to enchant armours.
Scroll of Enchant Accessory: used to enchant accessories.

Possible Results:
Enchantment will consume 1 Enchanting Scroll, and have 3 possible results:
1. Fail: Enchant failed, the weapon will be destroyed.
2. Success1: Enchant success but the enchantment level does not increase.
3. Success2: Enchant success and the enchantment level increases.

Safe Enchant:
When trying to enchant a piece of equipment whose enchant level is under safe enchant level, the equipment won't be destroyed upon failure.

Enchantment Effects:
Enchanting an armor can reduce the value of your AC (increase defense). Some may also increase your magic resistance.
Enchanting a weapon can increase the final attack damage. When reaching certain enchantment level, weapons will provide extra effect.
Enchanting an accessory will provide you extra HP, MP or other effects.

Type of Scrolls:
Enchantment srolls can be sorted as: Normal, Blessed, Cursed.
1.Normal Enchantment Scrolls
Chance to increase enchantment level by 1 upon success.
When enchantment level is over 8, has a chance to remain unchanged upon success.
2.Blessed Enchantment Scrolls
Chance to increase different enchantment level upon success according to different types of equipment.
a. Blessed Scroll of Weapon Enchantment:
When enchantment level is 1 to 3, chance to increase enchantment level by 1 to 3 upon success;
When enchantment level is 4 to 6, chance to increase enchantment level by 1 to 2 upon success;
No extra effects when enchanting an item with an enchantment level 7 or above.
b. Blessed Scroll of Armor Enchantment:
When enchanting an armor with an enchantment level of 1 to 2, has a chance to increase the enchantment level from 1 to 3 upon success.
When enchanting an armor with an enchantment level of 3 to 5, has a chance to increase the enchantment level from 1 to 2 upon success.
No extra effects when enchanting an item with an enchantment level 6 or above.
3.Cursed Enchantment Scrolls
Decreases enchantment level by 1 upon success.

Scroll of Protection
Function:
A Scroll of Protection can protect an item from being destoryed when an enchanting process fails, but the enchantment level of this item will become 0.
Types:
There are different types of scrolls: Scroll of Protection, Scroll of Accessory Protection, and Advanced Scroll of Weapon Protection.
Limitation:
Scroll of Protection cannot be used when it is within the armour safe enchant.
Scroll of Protection can be used on both weapons and armours.
Scroll of Accessory Protection can only be used on accessories.
Advanced Scroll of Weapon Protection can only be used on weapons with enchantment levels of level 12 or below. It can also limit the enchantment level deduction up to -3 when the enchanting process fails.
