HP Gauge
Shows character's HP. HP will be deducted when the character is under attack. If HP reaches 0, the character dies.
HP can recover naturally when the weight is lower than 50%.

MP Gauge
Shows character's MP. MP will be deducted when the character is casting spells.
MP can recover naturally when the weight is lower than 50%.

Level
Shows character's current level.

AC(Armor Class)
Shows character's armor class, and represents the defense against attacks. Lower AC means stronger defense.

Lawful
Shows character's Lawful from -32768 to 32767. Lawful shows if your character is good or evil. From -1000 to 1000 is Neutral State. From -32768 to -1001 is Chaos Status. Form 1001 to 32767 is Lawful State. Different state has different effects. Check [Game System-Lawful] for more details.

Weight
Item and equipment weight carried by a character is displayed in percentage.
Load Items Character is not able to naturally recover HP and MP when weight reaches 50%.
Character is not able to attack when your weight reaches 82%.

Experience
It is displayed as a bar at the bottom of the game interface. In character attribute interface, it is dispalyed in percentage(%).
Level up when it reaches 100%.

Location
Shows current map/region name and coordinate of where the character is.
