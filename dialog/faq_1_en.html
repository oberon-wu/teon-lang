HP Calculation
Shows character's HP. HP will be deducted when the character is under attack. If hit points reach 0, the character dies.
HP can be recovered when the weight is lower than 50%.

MP Calculation
Shows character's MP. MP will be deducted when the character is casting spells.
MP can be recovered when the weight is lower than 50%.

Level
Shows current character's level.

AC(Armor Class)
Shows character's armor class, and represents the defense against attacks from monsters. Lower AC means stronger defense.

Karma
Shows character's Karma from -32768 to 32767. Karma shows if your character is good or evil. From -1000 to 1000 is Neutral State. From -1001 to -32768 is Chaos Status. Form 1001 to 32767 is Lawful State. Different state has different effects. Check [Game System-Karma] for more details.

Weight
Item and equipment weight carried by a character is displayed in percentage.
Load Items Your HP/MP will not regenerate automatically when your weight is over 50%.
You are not able to attack when your weight is or over 82%.

Experience
It is displayed as a bar at the bottom of the game interface. In character attribute interface, it is dispalyed in percentage(%).
Level up when it reaches 100%.

Location
Shows current map/region name and coordinate of where the character is.
