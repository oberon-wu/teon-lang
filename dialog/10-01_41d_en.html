Quest:
Defeat Shelob. Collect 10 Shelob Legs, 5 Orcish Wand and bring back to Barret.

Guide:
Barret(32812,32846) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the East.
Shelob and Orc can be found around Barret.
Description:
Shelob will prevent the Rebel Army from hunting more orcs in the future. Barret hope that you can help him to defeat them.
