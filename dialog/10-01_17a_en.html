Chaos State
1. Chaos State: Upon killing another player, the difference between your and your victim's Lawful Value will be reduced from that of yours.

2. Lawful Value: Players whose Lawful Value is between -1001 and -32768 belongs to the Chaos State. Their names are displayed in red.

3. Chaotic Penalties:
Your character's name is displayed in red so other players can identify you easily.

Some NPC in the town will not interact with characters of Chaos State.

Increased item drop rate (inventory) upon death.

Receive lower healing when being casted by heal spell.
