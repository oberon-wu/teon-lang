HP
Hit Points, also known as HP, are character's life. If hit points reach 0, the character dies.

MP
Mana Points, also known as MP, are character's magic energy. The main use of mana points is to cast spells.

Experience
Experience Points or XP are units that are used to gain Levels. It is displayed in percentages (%).

Level
Measure of the overall power of the player character and progress of the adventure. The more experienced the player is, the better the player becomes at both fighting and magic.

Armor Class(AC)
Armor class, or AC, represents the defense against attacks from monsters. Lower AC means stronger defense.

Lawful Value
Displays the alignment of a character. From -32768 (full chaotic) to 32767 (full lawful).

Weight
Item and equipment weight carried by a character is displayed in percentage under your HP/MP bars. Your HP/MP will not regenerate automatically when your weight is over 50%.

Chat Window
Located at the bottom of screen. Player can chat with other player in this window/

Clock
Located at the up-right corner of screen. Showing in-game time.

Hot Key
Located beside attack button. can be used as a shortcut.(Both skills & items)

Menu
Access to basic features such as Character, Bag, Skills, Quests, Social and Settings.
