In this game, any items or resources may have three different status: cursed, normal and blessed. This applies to every type of items.

Equipment Status
1. Cursed Equipment: Cannot be unequipped once put on unless using Scroll of Curse Removal.
2. Blessed Equipment: Provides additional beneficial effects. Please see Equipment part 2 and 3.

Enchantment Scrolls
1. Cursed Enchantment Scroll: Decreases enchantment level by 1 upon success.
2. Blessed Enchantment Scroll: Chance to increase enchantment level by 1 to 3 upon success.

Potions
1. Cursed Potion: Reduced healing effect or duration.
2. Blessed Potion: Increased healing effect or duration.
