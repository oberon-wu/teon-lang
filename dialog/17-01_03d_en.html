Quest Target:
Collect 1 Black Knight's Sword, and give it to the Mysterious Mage in Cassandra Home.

Target Guiding:
Mysterious Mage is in Cassandra Home on Promise Island.
Black Knight Leader appears in Sandwood Region.

Quest Description:
Tyrant Attila sent several Black Knight Leaders to Sandwood in order to capture the Resistances from Promise Island and Edin, and the representative of Resistances, King Arthur's Descent. Mysterious Mage wants you to eliminate them and clears the barriers that stop King Arthur's Descent from taking back the throne.
