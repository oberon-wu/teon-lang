Click the [ï¸¾] icon under your character protrait and open the menu. Inside the menu, you can access to basic features such as Character, Backpack, Polymorph, Skill, Quest, Social, Clan and General(In-game setting).
The menu and any sub-interface are movable. You can tap, hold and drag a window such as character, backpack, and magic to anywhere you want so as to open it directly..

Character
To check character attribute, equipment, backpack and achievement.
Attribute: Shows each attribute status of the character.
Equipment: Shows the equipment worn on the character and can unequip by double-clicking the selected equipment. But wearing equipment is not supported in this interface.
Backpack: Open character's backpack and do operation to the items in the backpack.

Backpack
Access to the character backpack. You can check or use the item inside the backpack, check the amount of Gold and current weight of the character. Also, you can destroy, lock or sort the items inside the backpack.
For more details about destroy, lock and sort operations, check [Interface - How to play]

Skill
Access to spell and skill. You can check the skill description and the skills you've learned.
Normal Skill: General magic.
Class Skill: Magic for each class.
Awakening Skill: The magic that can only be learned by Elf/Dark Elf after awakening.
Temporary Skill: Magic within the equipment.
Quest
Access to quest bar that allows you to check the quest details.
In progress: shows the quests that have been accepted or are acceptable by players. Accepted quests will be displayed first.
Unacceptable Quest: Shows the quests that player is unqualified to accept.
Completed Quest: Shows the quests have been completed.

Social
Access to a social interface that allows you to do operations to team up add friends or blacklist.
Party: You can check other party members' titles, clans and classes. You can also do other operations to your members. For example, add friends, have a private chat, kick out members or leave the party.
Friends: You can see whether your friend is online or not through the friend column. You can also check your friend's clan, title, class and have a private chat with your friend or delete your friend.
Join a Clan: Submit an application to the clan where he/she belongs to. If he/she is the clan chief, you can directly send a face-to-face application.
Blacklist: Block the people inside the name list and can block the chat of the characters on any channel.

Clan
Access to clan interface. The contents are different depending on whether you join a clan or not:
Non-clan Player: Clan list will be displayed, and you can check the clan list, select a clan and apply to join.
Clan Player: Clan interface you belong to will be displayed. Clan chief can manage the clan, assign clan rewards or collect clan welfare. Clan members can check other members' profiles, add friends and collect clan welfare.
For more details related to clan, check [System - Clan]

General
In this interface, you can set the game sound effects, screen settings, battle settings, system settings and so on.
Sound Effects: You can adjust the background music volume and sound effects volume.
Screen: You can turn on/off the surrounding effects.
Battle: You can turn on/off the attacked alert, attacked direction, attacked effects and revenge mode.
Revenge Mode: Attack the red name status player without opening the PK mode.
System: You can adjust the screen, lock the hotkeys, set multi-touch, movement, reset hotkey and so on.
General: You can turn on/off team up, trade, elf night version, level display and some other settings.
Leave: You can restart(back to choose character), return to server selection, leave the game or log out.
