Definition
Value that shows if the character is good or evil.
When you kill another player, NPC in a village or a town guard, your Karma will tend to the Chaos Status;
Hunting monsters will increase your Karma and thus bring you to the Lawful State;
Newly created characters belong to the Neutral State.

Neutral State
Newly created characters belong to the Neutral State;
Players whose Karma is between -1000 and +1000 belong to neutral state;
ID will be displayed in white.

Lawful State
Lawful State
Players earn Karma when killing monsters. The Karma rewarded by different monsters are distinct.
Karma
Players whose Karma is between +1001 and +32767 belong to Lawful State. ID will be displayed in blue.
Rewards
Those with higher Karma have lower chance to drop items upon death and receive higher healing when using a healing magic.

Chaos Status
Evil State
Upon killing another player, the difference between your and the victim's Karma will be reduced from that of yours.
Karma
Players whose Karma is between -1001 and -32768 belong to Evil State. ID will be displayed in red.
Penalty
Your character's name is displayed in red so other players can identify you easily.
Some NPCs in the village will not interact with characters of Chaos Status.
Increased item drop rate upon death.
Decreased healing effect from healing magics. Effects of evil magic spell will be enchanted.
