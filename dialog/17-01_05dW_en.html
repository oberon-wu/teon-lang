Quest Target:
Bring 4 Lump of Gold to Upton [Armor Shop].

Target Guiding:
Upton [Armor Shop] is in Damelot Town.

Quest Description:
Upton thinks that you are not qualified enough to let him build the King Arthur's Holy Weapon(Replica). But he can make a copy of your Mage Class Certification. Bring Lump of Gold x4 to Upton [Armor Shop], and ask him to build a imitation of Mage class certification for you.
