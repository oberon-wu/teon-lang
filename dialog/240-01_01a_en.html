Since Sandwood has turned into a desert, the monsters began occupying Damelot Dungeon.
For many years we have been trying to defeat the monsters, but no matter what, we can't kill them.
Now we also use the Damelot Dungeon as a trial place for warriors. To become a true warrior, you need to survive in the dungeon.

First, head to Damelot Dungeon 1F to defeat Bladeling and collect Blade of Bladeling.
