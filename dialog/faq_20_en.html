Types
In Teon, every items or resources may have three different status: cursed, normal and blessed.

Effects
Different status of items have different effects.

Equipment
Cursed Equipment: Cannot be unequipped once equipped unless using Scroll of Curse Removal.
Blessed Equipment: Provides additional beneficial effects. For more details, please see [Systems - Equipment - Equipment Status]

Enchantment Scroll
For more details, please see [Systems - Equipment Enchant - Enchantment Scrolls Types]

Potions
Cursed Potion: Reduced healing effect or duration.
Blessed Potion: Increased healing effect or duration.
