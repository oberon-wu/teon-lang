If you are lost while looking for someone, you can open the Map by tapping the Location Icon on the top left of your screen .
Inside the Map, you can activate the navigation pointer to guide you to a specific location of using the Direction button under the NPC name.

You can also Bookmark a Location and use a Scroll of Teleportation (Blessed) to teleport there instantly.
