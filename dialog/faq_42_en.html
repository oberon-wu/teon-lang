Suggest 1

Advantage: High long range damage and AC
Disadvantage: Low HP and magic resistance
Tips: Ranged build, suitable for wind-element elves, can choose to strengthen Ranged Damage or magic ability or magic resistance to continue in this path.


Suggest 2

Advantage: High spell power.
Disadvantage: Low magic resistance, AC, ranged damage
Tips: Magic build, suitable for those who learn fire, earth, water spells, encouraged to balance with physical damage and healing.


Suggest 3

Advantage: High HP
Disadvantage: Low magic resistance, AC, ranged damage
Tips: Flexible build, wind elf will increase its Ranged damage, fire elf will increase its Melee damage, water and earth elf will increase its dodge ability.


Suggest 4

Advantage: Balanced melee damage and magic damage
Disadvantage: Low HP, magic resistance, AC and Ranged damage
Tips: Semi-magic build, suitable for those who learn fire and earth elements, able to have a higher HP and defence during the late gameplay, a build for Melee Damage combine with magic.


Suggest 5

Advantage: Balanced ranged damage and magic damage
Disadvantage: Low HP and magic resistance
Tips: Balance build, suitable for those who learn wind and earth elements, will be able to strengthen both Spell Power and Ranged damage during the late gameplay.


Suggest 6

Advantage: High magic resistance
Disadvantage: Low AC, spell power and ranged damage
Tips: Resist Magic build, wind elf will increase its Ranged damage, fire elf will increase its Melee damage, water and earth elf will increase its dodge ability.
