Quest:
Defeat Lizardman collect 10 Skin of Lizardman and bring back to Granny Olan.

Guide:
Granny Olan is in Sandwood Town.
Lizardman is to the Northwest of Sandwood Town.

Description:
Granny Olan is preparing supplies to fight against the coldand ask you to collect Lizardman Skin for her. In return, she will tell you a story.
