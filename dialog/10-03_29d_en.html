Quest:
Defeat Deceased Villager. Collect Family Token x6 for Gordon.

Guide:
Gordon(32675,32830) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the southwest.
Deceased Villager can be found around Gordon.

Description:
For the safety of the villagers, Gordon asks you to defeat the Deceased Villager.
You should use Scroll of Polymorph, so that they will not actively attacks you.
