The Scout Daily Quest is mean to help the people who lived in the Escon Town. And as exchange rewards, the Eva Guardian will received rewards from the peeple in the Escon Town.

Before the Eva Guardian can start to help in this quest series, you need to finish the Quest: Assist Escon's Defense which can be accepted from Vice President of Hunter Guild who is in the Maros Town.

1. The Eva Guardian is set for the purpose of protecting the people in the Escon Town;

2. After completed the Quest: Assist Escon's Defense, Eva Guardian can accept the daily quest from the corresponding level of Scout Quest Delegator in Escon Town;

1) The daily quests provide EXP and Eva Guardian Badge as rewards;
2) The daily quests are repeatable;
3) The daily quests are divided into Lv.1, Lv.2, and Lv.3;
4) You have to submit one corresponding permit to start the daily quest;
5) The Scout Permits are sold by the corresponding permit vendor next to the Scout Quest Delegator;
6) Each Scout Permit has expired time, please start the quest before it expired.
