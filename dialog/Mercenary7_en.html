Mercenary rules:
1. Mercenaries will fight with their employer clan. The basic rules is the same as clan memeber.
2. Mercenaries' profile will be hiden, the name will become [Mer.+No.], i.e. [Mer.005].
3. Mercenaries are NOT clan member, clan related spell like [Brave Arua] will NOT affect mercenaries.
4. Mercenaries need to defeat enemies to receive rewards.
5. Situations listed below are NOT counted as defeat:
1) Defeat characters not participate in siege battle.
2) Defeat your employer clan's member.
3) Defeat other mercenaries in your employer clan.
4) Defeats before siege battle begins.
