Mercenary rules:
1. Mercenaries will fight with their employer clan. The basic rules are the same as clan members.

2. Mercenaries' profile will be hidden, the name will become [Mer.+No.], i.e. [Mer.005].

3. Mercenaries are NOT clan members, clan-related spells like [Brave Arua] will NOT affect mercenaries.

4. Mercenaries need to defeat enemies to receive rewards.

5. Situations listed below are NOT counted as defeat:
A. Defeated characters do not participate in a siege battle.
B. Defeated your employer clan's member.
C. Defeated other mercenaries in your employer clan.
D. Defeats before siege battle begins.
