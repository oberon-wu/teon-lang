Quest Target:

Target Guiding:
Rufus(32811,33581) is in Maros Town.
Thri Keen will appear at Maros Dungeon 3/F and Maros Dungeon 4/F.

Rufus is collecting materials for insect research. He will be glad to pay Gold and Items to exchange for Insect Saliva you collected.
