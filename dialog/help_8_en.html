Trade: In the game, players can trade in-game items and gold with other players freely. In Teon, many items and resources are tradable.

How to Trade
1. Start a trade: Tap on the player portrait and select Trade in the drop-down menu.
2. Add items: Tap and hold an item then drag it into the trade window.
3. Lock items: Once locked, the items in the trade window cannot be changed again. Now wait for the other party to lock their items.
4. Confirm Trade: When both parties have locked and checked the items in the trade window, they can complete the trade by taping on confirm button.
5 Cancel trade: Close the trade window to cancel the trade.

Important Information
1. Any trade completed in the game is irreversible. We strongly recommend players to use extra cautions while trading with others to avoid unnecessary losses
2. If the trade fails and your backpack is full, the overweighted items will drop to the ground.
