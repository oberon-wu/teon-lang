Quest:
Alcock gave you an Ultimate Scroll of Polymorph to shapeshift into a Demon Archer. Talk to him again as an Demon Archer.

Guide:
Alcock is in Medieval Field.Ultimate Scroll of Polymorph can be found in your bag.

Description:
Alcock asks you to use the Ultimate Scroll of Polymorph, to shapeshift into a Demon Archer.

Demon Archer is the strongest shape among all others and requires level 50 before you can fully control its power.
