Quest:
Find Arthur and talk to him.

Guide:
Arthur(32757,32818) is in Medieval Field.

Description:
Find Arthur.
1. Tap Main Menu (your Character Portrait), tap [Bag]
2. Tap armor to view its details, tap [Use] to equip it
3. You can also double tap an item to [Use] equip immediately
4. Armor comes in three classes: shirt, armor and cloak
