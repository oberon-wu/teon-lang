To enchant your Trainee's Armor, you will need a Scroll of Enchant Trainee's Armor. You can either buy it from me, or take some Ore of Lesser Enchantment to Doris [Armor Specialist] if you want to craft it.

Use a Scroll of Enchant Trainee's Armor to enchant any piece of your Trainee's Armor from +0 to +1. Once you are done, come back to talk to me again. I will then enchant one of your other piece from +0 to +1 as a reward to your effort.

You can bring me one of the +1 Trainee's Armor below to prove that you have learned how to enchant your armor:

+1 Trainee's Spike Leather Armor
+1 Trainee's Leather Cap
+1 Trainee's Cloak of Stealth
+1 Trainee's Shirt
+1 Trainee's Leather Gloves
+1 Trainee's Leather Boots
