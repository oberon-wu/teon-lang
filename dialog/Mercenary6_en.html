Become a mercenary:
1. Only characters not from the defense clan or a leader can become a mercenary.
2. You can go to "Check recruiting clan" interface to check clans that hiring mercenaries.
3. You can go to "Check employer clan" to check your employer.
4. If you are refused by a clan, you can not hired by this clan again during this siege battle.

Getting rewards:
1. When a siege battle finished and you had defeat at least 1 enemy, you can get your personal reward.
2. You can go to "Check your reward" to check your record in this siege battle and receive your reward.
3. Your record and reward will keep for 2 weeks.
