Quest Target:
Talk to Swain.


Target Guiding:
Swain is at Promise Island Dungeon 2/F.
You can go to Promise Island through the Boatman in the south of Maros Town.
After arriving at Promise Island, you can find the entrance of Promise Island Dungeon through Minimap.


Quest Description:
Royal Class Trainer asks you to go to Promise Island Dungeon 2/F and find Swain.
