1.Jokul & Hunting Mountain entrance limitation:
Jokul: twice per day
Hunting Mountain: once per day

2.Jokul & Hunting Mountain require Jokul Permit or Hunting Mountain Permit to access.

3.Permits of Jokul & Hunting Mountain are contained in the Jokul & Hunting Mountain Stone Box. Which can be redeemed from Ivanna [Crystal Hunter] in Dartford Town.
Hunting Mountain Stone Box: Can be redeemed by character who’s level between 47 and 54 with 10,000 gold and 1 Hunting Mountain Admission Certificate. And character who’s level above 54 with 30,000 gold and 1 Hunting Mountain Admission Certificate.
Jokul Stone Box: Can be redeemed by character who’s level between 47 and 54 with 120 TC and 1 Jokul Admission Certificate. And character who’s level above 54 with 120 TC and Jokul Admission Certificate.

4.Both Stone Boxes have an expiration date of 72 hours and will disappear after used or expired.

5.Both Stone Boxes contained corresponding permits, and the permits have an expiration date of 30 mins. Both permits are multiple usage within the expiration date and teleport your character into corresponding maps after using the permit. Character will be teleported back to Dartford after the permit expires.

6.Using conditions and limitations for each permit:
Hunting Mountain Stone Box: Only obtainable for characters who've reached Lv. 47 and finished the main quest series of Monster Hunter. Using the Hunting Mountain Permit will teleport the character to Hunting Mountain Lv.1 when level between 47 - 54, and teleport who’ve character level above 54 to Hunting Mountain Lv.2.
Jokul Stone Box: Only obtainable for characters who’ve reached Lv.47 and finished the main quest series of Monster Hunter. Using the Jokul Permit will teleport the character to Jokul Lv.1 when level between 47 - 54, and teleport who’ve character level above 54 to Jokul Lv.2.

7.Dropping and EXP Gaining:
Hunting Mountain Lv.1: Having chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal and Red Crystal. Lower monsters’ EXP. Gold will not drop from monsters.
Hunting Mountain Lv.2: Having a higher chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal and Red Crystal compared to Hunting Mountain Lv.1. Lower monsters’ EXP. Gold will not drop from monsters.
Jokul Lv.1: Having a chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal and Red Crystal. Having a chance to drop Blue Crystal. Lower monsters’ EXP. Gold will not drop from monsters.
Jokul Lv.2: Having a higher chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal and Red Crystal compared to Jokul Lv.1. Having a chance to drop Blue Crystal. Lower monsters’ EXP. Gold will not drop from monsters.

8.Both Stone Boxes are non-tradable. Please redeem that as your need. Admission Certificates can be purchased from Ivanna [Crystal Hunter].

9.Crystal Prize Rules:
There is a chance to acquire Red Crystal from hunting monster in all crystal hunting maps.
There is a chance to acquire Blue Crystal from hunting monster in Jokul Lv.1 and Lv.2.
Red Crystal and Blue Crystal can be use to attend Crystal Prize.
Red Crystal Prize:Every Red Crystal Prize consumes Red Crystal x15, and acquire 1 random Red Crystal Prize reward and 1 Red Crystal Prize Point.
Blue Crystal Prize:Every Blue Crystal Prize consumes Blue Crystal x15, and acquire 1 random Blue Crystal Prize reward and 1 Blue Crystal Prize Point.
Prize Points:
Point can be uesd to redeem rewards;
Each Prize acquire 1 Point;
Each redeem consumes 15 Points.
10.Reward Introduction:
15 Red Crystal Prize Point can redeem Blue Crystal(Non-tradable) x1.
15 Blue Crystal Prize Point can redeem Advanced Hero Emblem(Tradable) x1.
