Contents：
1.Ymir & Jokul Certificate purchases limitation:
Ymir: once per day
Jokul: twice per day

2.Ymir & Jokul require Ymir Permit or Jokul Permit to access.

3.Permits of Ymir & Jokul are contained in the Ymir & Jokul Stone Box. Which can be redeemed from Ivanna [Crystal Hunter] with Certificate in Dartford Town.
Ymir Stone Box:
Can be redeemed by character who’s level between 47 and 54 with 2,000 gold and 1 Ymir Certificate. Can be redeemed by character who’s level above 54 with 5,000 gold and 1 Ymir Certificate.
Jokul Stone Box:
Can be redeemed by character who’s level above 47 with 120 TC and 1 Jokul Certificate.

4.Both Stone Boxes will be expired in 72 hours and will disappear after used or expired. Both Certificate will be expired in 6 hours.

5.Both Stone Boxes contain corresponding permits. Using permits will teleport your character to corresponding maps. Permit will not consume but will expire after 30 mins and teleport your character back to Dartford Town.

6.Conditions and Limitations:
Both maps are only avaliable for characters who've reached Lv. 47 and finished the main quest series of Monster Hunter.
Ymir:
Using the Ymir Permit will teleport the character to Ymir Lv.1 when level between 47 - 54, and teleport the character to Ymir Lv.2 when level above 54.
Jokul:
Using the Jokul Permit will teleport the character to Jokul Lv.1 when level between 47 - 54, and will teleport the character to Jokul Lv.2 when level above 54.

7.Dropping and EXP Gaining:
Monsters provide less EXP and drop no gold in these areas.
Ymir Lv.1: Having chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal and Red Crystal.
Ymir Lv.2: Having a higher chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal and Red Crystal compared to Ymir Lv.1.
Jokul Lv.1: Having a chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal, Red Crystal and Blue Crystal.
Jokul Lv.2: Having a higher chance to drop Apocalyptic Source Crystal, Red Crystal and Blue Crystal compared to Jokul Lv.1.

8.Certificates, Stone Boxes, and Permits are non-tradable. Please acquire as you need. Certificates can purchase from Ivanna [Crystal Hunter].
