Here’s the detailed info of the Event.

The Event Time: 19:00:00 (PDT) 06-07-2021 to 09:00:00 (PDT) 06-22-2021

Special Item Dropping Time: 16:00:00 - 18:00:00 (PDT) & 04:00:00 - 06:00:00 (PDT) during the Event Time

Redeem Time: 19:00:00 (PDT) 06-07-2021 to 19:00:00 (PDT) 06-29-2021

Event contents:
1.During the Special Item Dropping Time period, adventurers can get a new item, the Sticky Rice, dropping from monsters in Maros areas.

2.The Sticky Rice is only dropping in the Special Item Dropping Time period which are 16:00:00 - 18:00:00 (PDT) & 04:00:00 - 06:00:00 (PDT) during the The Event Time of 19:00:00 (PDT) 06-07-2021 to 09:00:00 (PDT) 06-22-2021.

3.Adventurers can redeem special gifts in the redeem list after collecting enough Sticky Rice. And the redeemed NPC is the Event Emissary.

4.For adventurers who want to make the special gift to become permanent, you can tap the redeem list in the Event Emissary and redeem it with the requirement listed.

5.All the time-limited items that redeemed have the expiration date of 48 hours.

6.All the Sticky Rice will expire at 19:00:00 (PDT) on 06-29-2021.

7.The NPC of the event, the Event Emissary, will be gone when the Event finishes.
