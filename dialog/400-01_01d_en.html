Quest Target:
Bring one Ring of Heroism to the Alchemist.

Target Guiding:
Alchemist is in the west outside the Maros Town.
Ring of Heroism can be obtained from Dellas[Hero].

Quest Description:
The Alchemist has some troubles with the alchemy research direction. He hopes you could bring some special golden accessories to him. Ring of Heroism would be a good choice.
