Quest Target:
Replace all your armor with Trainee's Armor and speak with Rabia [Armor Shop] again.

Target Guiding:
Rabia [Armor Shop] is in Maros Town.

Quest Description:
Rabia is instructing to you on how to improve your armor. Your first step is to get yourself a whole set of Trainee's Armor, which includes a Helmet, a Doublet, a Body Armor, a Cloak, Gloves, Shoes and perhaps a Shield if you are ask for it. If you are not sure what kind of Trainee's Armor you are required, you should speak with Rabia [Armor Shop] again and find out.
