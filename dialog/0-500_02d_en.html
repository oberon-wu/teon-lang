Quest:
Talk to Milon again once you have equipped the weapon in your [Bag].

Guide:
Milon is in Medieval Field.

1. Tap Main Menu, tap [Bag]
2. Tap your weapon to view its details, tap [Use] to equip it

3.You can also double tap an item to [Use] equip immediately

Description:
Milon has gave you a weapon. Make sure the weapon is equiped and talk to him again.
