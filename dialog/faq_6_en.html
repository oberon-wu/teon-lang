Movement
You can move your character by tapping on the ground;
or using the move pad by swiping the screen.

Menu Instructions
The menu and any sub-interface are movable. You can tap, hold and drag a window such as character, backpack, and magic to anywhere you want.

Object Movement
Objects such as magics and items can be put to your action-buttons. To do so, tap on an object, hold and drag to the button you want to use.

View
You can tap on an item, equipment or magic to inspect the detailed information.

How to Use
You can double-tap an item to use it. For example, double-tap to drink a potion, equip gear, or cast a magic.

About Backpack
1. Adjust
In your backpack, you can tap, hold and drag an item or equipment to the desired slot.
2. Destroy
In your backpack, you can click [Destroy] - select the item you want to destroy - click [Complete], then the selected item will be destroyed.
3. Lock
In your backpack, click [Lock] - select the item you want to lock - click [Complete], then the selected item will be locked. Locked item cannot be used.\
To unlock an item, click [Lock] again - select the item you want to unlock - click [Complete], then the selected item will be unlocked.
4. Sort
Click [Sort] button in your backpack and all the items will be sorted automatically in a specific order.
