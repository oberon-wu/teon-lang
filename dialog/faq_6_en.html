Movement
You can move your character by tapping on the ground;
or using the move pad by swiping the screen.

Menu Instructions
All sub-menus and interface are movable. You can drag and drop them, such as Character, Bag, or Spell to wherever you want.

Object Movement
Objects such as spells and items can be put on your action-buttons. To do so, drag and drop them to the button you want to use.

View
You can tap on an item, equipment or spell to inspect the detailed information.

How to Use
You can double-tap an item to use it. For example, double-tap to drink a potion, equip gear, or cast a spell.

About Bag
1. Adjust
In your bag, you can drag and drop an item or equipment to the desired slot.
2. Discard
In your bag, you can click [Discard] - select the item you want to destroy - click [Done], then the selected item will be discarded. Discarded items cannot be restored.
3. Lock
In your bag, click [Lock] - select the item you want to lock - click [Done], then the selected item will be locked. Locked item cannot be used.
To unlock an item, click [Lock] again - select the item you want to unlock - click [Done], then the selected item will be unlocked.
4. Sort
Click [Sort] button in your bag and all the items will be sorted automatically in a specific order.
