Equipment Enchantment is a crucial way to fortify your character. Enchanted gear provides additional benefits such as increased damage, accuracy and resistances.
1. Enchanting Materials: Weapon or Armor Enchantment Scrolls, Scroll of Enchant Accessory.
2. Threshold: When trying to enchant a piece of equipment whose condition is under the threshold, the equipment won't be destroyed upon failure.
3. Possible Results: Fail; Success 1; Success 2.
Fail: If the enchanting attempt fails, the Enchanting Scroll will be consumed. If the equipment's condition is beyond the threshold, it will be destroyed.
Success 1: Enchant success but the enchantment level does not increase.
Success 2: Enchant success and the enchantment level increases.
4. Type of Scrolls: Blessed Enchantment Scrolls, Cursed Enchantment Scrolls.
Blessed Enchantment Scroll: Chance to increase enchantment level by 1 to 3 upon success.
Cursed Enchantment Scroll: Decreases enchantment level by 1 upon success.
4.1 Enchanting Equipment (Regular and Blessed)
Enchantment level may have a chance to remaind the same even if the enchanting process was successful when the item enchantment level is equal or greater than 8.
4.2 Blessed Scroll of Weapon Enchantment
When enchanting a weapon with an enchantment level of 1 to 3, has a chance to increase the enchantment level from 1 to 3 upon success.
When enchanting a weapon with an enchantment level of 4 to 6, has a chance to increase the enchantment level from 1 to 2 upon success.
No extra effects when enchanting an item with an enchantment level 7 or above.
4.3 Blessed Scroll of Armor Enchantment
When enchanting an armor with an enchantment level of 1 to 2, has a chance to increase the enchantment level from 1 to 3 upon success.
When enchanting an armor with an enchantment level of 3 to 5, has a chance to increase the enchantment level from 1 to 2 upon success.
No extra effects when enchanting an item with an enchantment level 6 or above.
5. Enchantment Effects
Enchanting an armor can reduce the value of your AC (increase defense). Some may also increase your magic resistance.
Enchanting a weapon can increase the final attack damage. When reach certain enchantment level weapons will provide extra effect.
Enchating an accessory will provide you extra HP, MP or other effects.
6. Scroll of Protection
A Scroll of Protection can protect an item from being destroyed when an enchanting process fails, but the enchantment level of this item will become 0.
A Scroll of Protection cannot be used on an item within the Safe Enchant Level.
There are different types of scrolls: Scroll of Protection, Scroll of Accessory Protection, and Advanced Scroll of Weapon Protection.
Scroll of Protection can be used on both weapons and armors.
Scroll of Accessory Protection can only be used on accessories.
Advanced Scroll of Weapon Protection can only be used on weapons with enchantment levels of level 12 or below. It can also limit the enchantment level deduction up to -3 when the enchanting process fails.
