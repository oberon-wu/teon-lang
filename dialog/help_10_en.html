Random Teleportation
1. You can teleport via items or skills. The destination cannot be selected.
2. Item: Scroll of Teleportation.
3. Skill: Teleport.

Controlled Teleport
1. How to teleport: Use the teleportation item or skill then select a destination.
2. Item: Blessed Scroll of Teleportation.
3. Skill: Use the skill Teleport while wearing a Ring of Teleportation.
4. Bookmarks: Provides the coordination of locations.
