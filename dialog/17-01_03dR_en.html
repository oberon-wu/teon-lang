Quest Target:
Collect 1 Spear of Dungeon Slaughterer and 1 Spear of Tunnel Slaughterer. Give them to the Mysterious Mage in Cassandra Home.

Target Guiding:
Mysterious Mage is in Cassandra Home on Promise Island.
Dungeon Slaughterer is at Promise Island Dungeon 2/F.
Tunnel Slaughterer is at Ancient Tunnel.

Quest Target:
Due to his mistake, Mysterious Mage summoned Baphomet. Now, the dark forces want to re-summon it back in the world. Mysterious Mage needs you to go to Promise Island Dungeon 2/F and Ancient Tunnel to beat the Dungeon Slaughterer and Tunnel Slaughterer so as to prevent the expansion of the dark forces.
