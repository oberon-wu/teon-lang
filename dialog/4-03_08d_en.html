Quest:
Bring 2 Enraged Bear hearts to Tiago.

Guide:
Bewitched Enraged Bears are On the Northwest of Maros.
Tiago (32839,33541) [Maros Town] is in Northwestern Maros Town.

Description:
Tiago wants to study how the dead souls are bewitching the Enraged Bear in Maros Dungeon. He needs you to gather 2 Enraged Bear hearts for him to do so and will give you a Void Stone for them.
