Quest:
Defeat Dwarf and Dwarf Warrior. Collect 10 Fire Vine and 10 Dwarven Potion and bring back to Gordon.

Guide:
Gordon(32675,32830) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the Southwest.
Dwarf and Dwarf Warrior can be found northwest from Gordon.

Description:
Gordon ask you to defeat Dwarf and Dwarf Warrior around him and collect 10 Fire Vine and 10 Dwarven Potion to him.
