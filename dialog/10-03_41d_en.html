Quest:
Defeat Invading Orc Ritualist. Collect Orcish Wand x6 and bring back to Barret.

Guide:
Barret(32812,32846) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the east.
Invading Orc Ritualist can be found around Barret.
Description:
Invading Orc Ritualist will prevent the Defenders from defeating more orcs in the future. Barret hope that you can help him to defeat them.
