Quest:
Defeat Lesser Island Shelob and Invading Orc Ritualist. Collect Shelob Legs x10, Orcish Wand x5 and bring back to Barret.

Guide:
Barret(32812,32846) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the East.
Lesser Island Shelob and Invading Orc Ritualist can be found around Barret.
Description:
Lesser Island Shelob will prevent the Defenders from defeating more orcs in the future. Barret hope that you can help him to defeat them.
