Quest Target:
Collect 1 Beleth Shadow Wand, and give it to the Mysterious Mage in Cassandra Home.

Target Guiding:
Mysterious Mage is in Cassandra Home on Promise Island.
Beleth Shadow is near the Sandwood Oasis.

Quest Description:
Beret who had fought against Great Mage for many years didn't disappear. His shadow come from the residue of his dark magic always appears near the Sandwood Oasis. Mysterious Mage hopes you can defeat Beleth Shadow so as to help royal retake the support of people of Edin.
