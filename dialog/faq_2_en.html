Display Location
Game time icon is located at the top right of the game interface.

Game Time
Every 4 hours in real world are equal to 1 day in the game.

Night Setting
During the night, the player's vision will be affected by the changing lighting condition.
During the night, you can use lighting tools such as candles and lanterns to extend your view distance.
Elves have night vision. During the night, an Elf's vision will turn to green. You can turn this feature off in [Settings].
During the night, undead creatures become more active and deal increased damage.
