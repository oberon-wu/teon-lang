Quest:

Guide:
Gerome(32814,32835) is to the East of Promise Island Town.
Skeleton type monster can be found at Promise Island Dungeon.

Description:
Gerome is collecting the Bone Set and asks you to collect Bone Armor and Armor Fragments for him.
