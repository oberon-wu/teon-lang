Quest:
Defeat Island Lycanthrope and Island Werewolf Fighter. Collect Wolf Pelt x8 and Alchemist Stone x1 for Drew.

Guide:
Drew(32785,32658) is near the Port to the north of Promise Island.
Island Lycanthropes and Island Werewolf Fighters can be found at northwest from Drew.

Description:
The followers of tyrant Attila arrived in Promise Island Town and they occupied the dock. Defeat them to protect the people in Promise Island.
