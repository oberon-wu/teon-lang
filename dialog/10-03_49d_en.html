Quest:
Defeat Island Lycanthrope and Island Werewolf Fighter. Collect Lycanthrope Fur x15 and Alchemist Stone x1 for Drew.

Guide:
Drew(32785,32658) is near the Port to the North of Promise Island.
Island Lycanthropes and Island Werewolf Fighters can be found at Northwest from Drew.

Description:
Casey has brought in the Island Lycanthropes and Island Werewolf Fighters, the fierce and yet intelligent monsters. Defeat them to protect the people in Promise Island.
