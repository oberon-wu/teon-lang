Adventurer Growth Reward Categories: In Adventurer Growth Reward, the Basic Rewards are unlocked as default option. Adventurers can use TC to unlock the Advanced Adventurer Growth Reward to acquire more rewards.

Adventurer Growth Reward Level: The Adventurer Growth Reward Level is based on adventurers' character level. Character can acquire the rewards when the character reached the corresponding level.

Adventurer Growth Reward Price: 400 TC
