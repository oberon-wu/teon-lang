Karma shows if your character is good or evil. When you kill another player, NPC in a village or a town guard, your Karma will tend to the Chaos State. Hunting monsters will increase your Karma and thus bring you to the Lawful State. Newly created characters belong to the Neutral State.
Neutral State
1. Newly created characters belong to the Neutral State. Their names are displayed in white.

Lawful State
1. Players earn Karma when killing monsters. The Karma rewarded by different monsters are distinct.
2. Karma: Players whose Karma is between +1001 and +32767 belong to the Lawful State. Their names are displayed in blue.
3. Rewards: Those with higher Karma have lower chance to drop items upon death and receive higher healing when using a healing magic.

Chaos State
1. Chaos State: Upon killing another player, the difference between your and the victim's Karma will be reduced from that of yours.
2. Karma: Players whose Karma is between -1001 and -32768 belong to the Chaos State. Their names are displayed in red.
3. Penalties:
Your character's name is displayed in red so other players can identify you easily.
Some NPCs in the village will not interact with characters of Chaos State.
Increased item drop rate upon death.
Decreased healing effect from healing magics.
