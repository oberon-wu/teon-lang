Quest Target:
Give 2 Orichalcum to Orichalcum Collector.

Target Guiding:
Orichalcum Collector is in Dartford Town.


Quest Description:
Trade Union is delegating qualified adventurers to collect Orichalcum. You can give 2 Orichalcum to Orichalcum Collector so as to exchange for experience, Advanced Hero Potion and EXP Cube(120% EXP Buff).
