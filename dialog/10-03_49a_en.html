We have finally discovered the evil behind the recent activities of Undeads, Orcs and Goblins.

Casey, the former High Priest in Promise Island Town, was expelled few years ago by Dellas. Casey was suspected in practicing Dark Magic described in some of the books left behind by the Mysterious Mage, trying to manipulate monsters and conspired in necromancy.

The recent acts of the monsters showed high relevance to Casey's former practice.
