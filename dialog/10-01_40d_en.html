Quest:
Defeat Island Golem. Collect Lump of Iron (Quest Item) x3 and bring back to Barret.

Guide:
Barret(32812,32846) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the East.
Island Golem can be found North from Barret.

Description:
Barret is collecting the materials required to craft the equipment for the Rebel Army and hope that you can help him to collect Lump of Iron (Quest Item).
