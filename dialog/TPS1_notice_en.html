Teon Pass Season One Notice:
1. Teon Pass Season One(TP) level is based on the TP Points that each TP level needed.
2. Each TP level needs 1000 TP Points.
3. The TP Point Note can acquire from Basic daily quests and Advanced daily quests.
4. The TP Points can redeem from TP Point Note in Teon Pass interface.
5. Basic daily quests can be accepted by any character and without limitation.
6. Advanced daily quests are only available for adventurers who purchase the Advanced Teon Pass.
7. The Adv. in the Teon Pass interface is the unlock button for Advanced Teon Pass.
8. The acquirable TP Points rewards in Basic daily quest and the Advanced daily quest are different in each day.
9. Basic daily quests require characters to have items TP Basic Permit and TP Daily Quest Permit to activate the first step of the basic daily quest.
10. Advanced daily quests require characters to have items TP Advanced Permit and TP Advanced Daily Quest Permit to activate the first step of the advanced daily quest.
11. Both TP Daily Quest Permit and TP Advanced Daily Quest Permit are only available to purchase once per day and will be reset the purchase limit at 00:00 on Teon Server Time. (Teon Server Time is available on our official website at www.teonworld.com).
12. TP Daily Quest Permit can purchase from Nancy.
13. TP Advanced Daily Quest Permit can purchase from Alina.
14. The daily quest NPC will be reset at 00:00.
15. Basic daily quests can be accepted from Nancy which is located in Maros Town.
16. Advanced daily quests can be accepted from Alina which is located in Maros Town.
17. The duration of Teon Pass is from September 14th, 2021 to November 13th, 2021.
