Friends
Add friends
You can make many friends in the game;
Add your friends to your friend list to keep in touch with them;
To add friends, you need to click the player's character or select the character by target-switch button, and click his/her head to open the social menu;
By clicking [Add Friend] button in the menu, you can add him/her.
Function
After adding friend, you can chat with your friend easily by [Menu - Social - Friend].
Notic
There's a limit number of friends you may have. We recommend you to keep an eye on your friend list and clean it up from time to time.

Party
You can see other party members' HP.
EXP gain is shared among all party members.
Some special classes can provide beneficial effects.
Party EXP will be assigned to each member in proportion to their weight.
