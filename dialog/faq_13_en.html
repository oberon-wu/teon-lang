Friends
Add friends:
You can make many friends in the game;
Add your friends to your friend list to keep in touch with them;
To add friends, you need to click the player's character or select the character by target-switch button, and click the character's portrait to open the social menu;
By clicking [Add Friend] button in the menu, you can add this character to your friend list.
Function:
After adding friend, you can chat with your friend easily by [Menu - Social - Friend].
Notice:
There's a limited number of friends you can have. We recommend you to keep an eye on your friend list and clean it up from time to time.

Team
You can see other team members' HP.
EXP gain is shared among all team members.
Some special classes can provide beneficial effects.
Team EXP will be assigned to each member in proportion to their weight.
