Quest:
Retrieve the Pass of Sandwood Castle from one of the Harpies and bring it to Granny Olan.

Guide:
Granny Olan is in Sandwood Town.
Harpy can be found in the west from Sandwood Town.

Description:
One of the Harpies took Granny Olan's Pass of Sandwood. You need to take it back so that you can enter Sandwood Castle.
