Quest Target:
Find Lum in Orc Twon and learn more about the updated situation of the Orcs.

Target Guiding:
Lum(33159,33452) is in Orc Town.

Quest Description:
Find Lum in Orc Twon and learn more about the updated situation of the Orcs.
Orc Town is in the South-East of Land of Nature. From here to the South-Ease, pass through the Elven Garden and Orcish Region, then you can reach the Orc Town.
You can also use Scroll of Escape. Use it in the Land of Nature and you will be teleported to the Orcish Guild. Then, you can find the Teleporter and you are able to reach the Orc Town! Or you can also keep going to the South-West from the southwest gate of Orcish Guild, and you can also reach the Orc Town and find Lum.
