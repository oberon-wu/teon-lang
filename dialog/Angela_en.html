Equipment Magic Crystal can be used for crafting some accessories and one of the cloak and Ore of Lesser Enchantment can be used for exchange the Scroll of Enchant Trainee's Equipments.

Dissolve +4 Trainee's armor for Equipment Magic Crystal

Dissolve +0 Trainee's weapon for Ore of Lesser Enchantment
