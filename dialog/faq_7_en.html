Button sort
There are 16 action-buttons in total. The biggest button is the attack button. Around the attack button spread 12 customizable blank buttons. Additionally, there are 2 self-cast buttons and 1 target-switch button.

Attack button
Single-tap the button to start auto-attack. Tap again to cancel. If the target is friendly, tap the button to interact.

Blank button
You can drag the often used items, equipments or magics to these customizable buttons. It can be used on other selected targets.

Self-cast button
These buttons work in the same way with the blank buttons above. The only difference is that the self-cast buttons can only target the player himself.

Target-switch button
You can swipe to left to switch between enemy targets and swipe to right to switch between player's character. If you swipe upwards, your own character will be selected. Tap on the button to select a random target.

Tap and hold
You can tap and hold an action-button to move it to anywhere you want.

Click the arrow nearby the attack button
You can reveal the blank button and call hidden blank buttons. There are 12 blank buttons in total.

Reset action-buttons
You can restore the default settings through [Menu - General - System - Reset buttons].
