Quest:
Collect 10 Skin of Lizardman from Lizardman for Granny Olan.

Guide
Granny Olan is inside Sandwood Town.
Lizardman is in the south from Sandwood Town.

Description
Granny Olan needs some clothing for the cold nights in the desert town. Help her get some Skin of Lizardman. In return, she will tell you the history of Sandwood.
