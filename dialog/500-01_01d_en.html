Quest Target:
After upgrading yourself to level 47 and completing the Class Trial Quest, talk to Vice President of Hunter Guild.

Target Guiding:
Vice President of Hunter Guild is in Maros Town.

Quest Description:
Hunter Guild is a non-profit guild. They work for collecting various monsters' information and they provide the info with many materials and treasures that may drop in different area. Recently, the guild hope to recruit more people for defeating monsters and providing more detailed monsters' information for the new adventurers. Therefore, they invited you to join their guild. Before joining, Vice President of Hunter Guild needs you to reach to level 47, and complete your class trial quest.
