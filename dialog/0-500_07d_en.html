Quest:
Defeat Weaken Deadly Lotus. Collect 3 Deadly Lotus Petal, and bring back to Alcock.

Guide:
Alcock is in Medieval Field.
Weaken Deadly Lotus can be found at Alcock South and Southwest side.

Description:
Monsters which are more aggressive, will actively attack us humans.

Alcock wants you to defeat those aggressive Weaken Deadly Lotus, and bring back their petal to Alcock.
