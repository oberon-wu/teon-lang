At ancient times, Humans, Orcs, Elves, Undeads and Demons had raged war against each other.
Wars lasted for generations and generations, and the Humans won in the end. To celebrate the victory of the Ancient Wars, the Human Sages ordered a great festival to be held annually -- The New World Celebration.
At the day of the Celebration, there will be processions, plays, games, sports, and campfire feasts.
