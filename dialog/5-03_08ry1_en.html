Since King Arthur had been in charge for a long time, after gaining a certain prestige, his ambition continued to expand. He hoped that he could control the heaven and it angered the Gods. They sealed King Arthur into the unfinished to the Babel.

The knights were left leaderless and collapsed due to the internal power scramble.

Meanwhile, a huge change was taken place in Sandwood Region. The original green plain became a windy and dusty desert due to the dramatic changes of the weather.
