Quest:
Defeat Invading Dwarf Servants and Invading Dwarf Warriors. Collect Fire Vine x10 and Dwarven Potion x10 and bring back to Gordon.

Guide:
Gordon(32675,32830) is on the outside of Promise Island Town to the southwest.
Invading Dwarf Warriors and Invading Dwarf Servants can be found nouthwest from Gordon.

Description:
Gordon asked you to defeat Invading Dwarf Servants and Invading Dwarf Warriors around him and collect Fire Vine x10 and Dwarven Potion x10 to him.
