Quest Target:
Give 2 Lump of Mithril to Mithril Collector.

Target Guiding:
Mithril Collector is in Dartford Town.


Quest Description:
Trade Union is delegating qualified adventurers to collect Lump of Mithril. You can give 2 Lump of Mithril to Mithril Collector so as to exchange for experience, Advanced Hero Potion and EXP Cube(120% EXP Buff).
