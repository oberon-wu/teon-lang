Quest Target:
Enchant your Trainee's Weapon to +1 and speak with Pascal [Weapon Shop] again.

Target Guiding:
Pascal [Weapon Shop] is in Maros Town.

Quest Description:
Pascal is instructing to you on how to increase your damage. You need to get a Scroll of Enchant Trainee's Weapon and use it on your Trainee's Weapon. If you are not sure what kind of Trainee's Weapon you are supposed to be enchanting, you should speak with Pascal [Weapon Shop] again and find out.
